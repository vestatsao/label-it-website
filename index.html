Your energy label includes an official assessment of your property's energy efficiency and government registration (EP-Online). Additional detailed report can be requested for an option to explain the label, and recommendations for improving energy performance. Additional report can be provided in English or Traditional Chinese.

An energy label is valid for 10 years from the date of issue. If you're selling or renting your property within this period and already have a registered label, you don't need a new one. Once the 10 years expire or if you don't have a label, a new assessment is required.

Yes, in the Netherlands an energy label is legally required when selling or renting a residential property. It must be registered in the official government database. Selling or renting without a valid label can result in significant fines.

A certified energy specialist conducts a physical inspection of your property, evaluating factors such as building year, insulation, window type, heating, cooling and ventilation system. Based on this data and standardized calculation methods, the final energy label is determined and registered in the national database.
